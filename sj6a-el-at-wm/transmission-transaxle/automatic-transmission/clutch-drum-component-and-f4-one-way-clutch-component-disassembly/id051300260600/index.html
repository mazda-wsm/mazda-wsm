Disassembly Procedure

1.  Using a flathead screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000260

2.  Remove the clutch hub component from the C3 clutch piston component.

3.  Remove the retaining plate and the drive plate and the driven plate from the clutch hub component.

bsj6za00000821

4.  Remove the retaining plate from the C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000262

5.  Remove the thrust needle bearing from the clutch hub.

bsj6za00000211

6.  Remove the clutch hub from the C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000822

7.  Remove the thrust bearing, the bearing race and the thrust washer from clutch hub.

bsj6za00000264

8.  Remove the C1, C4 clutch component from the C2, C3 clutch drum.

bsj6za00000665

9.  Remove the bearing race from the C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000305

10.  Remove the clutch hub and F4 one-way clutch from the C1, C4 clutch component.

bsj6za00000823

11.  Remove the bearing race from the C1, C4 clutch component.

bsj6za00000307

12.  Remove the F4 one-way clutch from the clutch hub.

bsj6za00000308

13.  Using a flathead screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000144

14.  Remove the retaining plate (C1) and the drive plate (C1) and the driven plate (C1) from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000666

15.  Using a flathead screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000146

16.  Remove the retaining plate (C4) and the drive plate (C4) and the driven plate (C4) from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000667

17.  Place the SST on the seal plate and compress the piston return spring with a press.

bsj6za00000871

Caution•  Be careful not to expand the snap ring too much.

18.  Remove the snap ring using snap ring pliers.

19.  Remove the C4 clutch seal plate and the piston return spring from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000554

20.  Apply compressed air into the oil passage as shown in the figure and remove the C4 clutch piston and C1 clutch piston from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000564

Air pressure

392 kPa (4.02 kgf/cm2, 57 psi)

21.  When applying compressed air, shut the oil passages of the input shaft component.

22.  Remove the O-ring from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000565

23.  Remove the D-ring from the C1 clutch seal plate.

bsj6za00000555

24.  Remove the seal ring from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000153

25.  Using a flathead screwdriver, remove the snap ring (C3) from the C2, C3 clutch drum.

bsj6za00000154

26.  Using a flathead screwdriver, remove the snap ring (C2) from the C2, C3 clutch drum.

bsj6za00000155

27.  Remove the retaining plate (C2) and the drive plate (C2) and the driven plate (C2) from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000669

28.  Install the SST as shown in the figure, and compress the C2 clutch piston return spring.

bsj6za00000888

Caution•  Be careful not to expand the snap ring too much.

29.  Remove the snap ring using snap ring pliers.

bsj6za00000787

30.  Remove the seal plate and the C2 clutch piston return spring from the C3 clutch drum.

bsj6za00000557

31.  Apply compressed air into the oil passage as shown in the figure and remove the C2 clutch piston and C3 clutch piston from the input shaft component.

bsj6za00000558

Air pressure

392 kPa (4.02 kgf/cm2, 57 psi)

32.  When applying compressed air, shut the oil passage of the input shaft component.

33.  Remove the O-ring from the C2 clutch seal plate.

bsj6za00000297

34.  Remove the O-ring from the C2 clutch piston.

bsj6za00000298

35.  Install the SST using a M10-1.25 bolt with a length of 100 mm as shown in the figure to compress the piston return spring.

bsj6za00000791

Caution•  Be careful not to expand the snap ring too much.

36.  Remove the snap ring using snap ring pliers.

bsj6za00000789

37.  Remove the seal plate piston return spring from the C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000300

38.  Remove the C2, C3 clutch drum C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000301

39.  Remove the O-ring from the C3 clutch piston component.

bsj6za00000302

40.  Remove the O-ring from the C2, C3 clutch drum.

bsj6za00000303
