DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059854}

• Power retractable hardtop warning buzzer sounding continuously while the power retractable hardtop is stopped. {: #wp1059857}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059861}

• Power retractable hardtop control module sends an improper buzzer operation signal to the instrument cluster. {: #wp1059864} - Power retractable hardtop control module malfunction {: #wp1059932}- Power retractable hardtop control module cannot detect the power retractable hardtop permission condition signal. {: #wp1059946} • Improper vehicle speed detected {: #wp1059956}• Improper transmission position detected (except for transmission neutral (MT)/N or P position (AT) detected) {: #wp1059970} • Power retractable hardtop control module detects a system malfunction {: #wp1059982} • Instrument cluster malfunction (Buzzer sounds regardless of the instruction from the power retractable hardtop control module) {: #wp1059992}

Diagnostic Procedure

• When performing the asterisked (*) troubleshooting inspection, slightly shake the wiring harness and connectors while performing the inspection to determine whether poor contact points are the cause of any intermittent malfunction. If there is a problem, verify that the connectors, terminals and wiring harness are connected correctly and undamaged.
