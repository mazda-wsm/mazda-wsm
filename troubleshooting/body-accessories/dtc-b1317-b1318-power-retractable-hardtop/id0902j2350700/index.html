DTC B1317/B1318 [POWER RETRACTABLE HARDTOP]

id0902j2350700

{: #wp1059781}

DTC }{: #wp1060738

B1317 {: #wp1059868}

Battery power supply voltage increases (17.5 V or more) {: #wp1059870}

B1318 {: #wp1059922}

Battery power supply voltage decreases (less than 7.5 V) {: #wp1059925}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059784}

• If the power retractable hardtop control module power supply voltage is not within 7.5-17.5 V. {: #wp1059842} *Note{: #wp1059989} • The system recovery condition after detecting DTC B1317/1318 is that the battery voltage is in the 8.5-16.5 V range. Verify that the battery voltage is 8.5-16.5 V* when inspecting. {: #wp1060021}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059889}

• Open or short circuit in wiring harness between battery and power retractable hardtop control module {: #wp1059895} • ROOM 15 A fuse malfunction {: #wp1060059} • Power retractable hardtop control module malfunction {: #wp1060069} • Battery malfunction {: #wp1060079}
