NO.1 INSUFFICIENT AIR (OR NO AIR) BLOWN FROM VENTS

id070300800400

{: #wp1059776}

1 }{: #wp1060974

Insufficient air (or no air) blown from vents. {: #wp1059781}

DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059854}

• Problem with each vent and/or duct. {: #wp1059857} • Airflow mode does not change. {: #wp1059912}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059861}

• Malfunction in airflow mode actuator {: #wp1059864} • Malfunction in VENT mode system {: #wp1059944} • Malfunction in HEAT mode system {: #wp1059954} • Malfunction in DEFROSTER mode system {: #wp1059964}

Diagnostic procedure

STEP {: #wp1060088}

INSPECTION {: #wp1060090}

ACTION {: #wp1060094}

1 {: #wp1059982}

INSPECT AIRFLOW MODE ACTUATOR {: #wp1059984} • Inspect airflow mode actuator. {: #wp1060200} • Is it okay? {: #wp1060214}

Yes {: #wp1060040}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060043}

No {: #wp1060123}

Repair or replace malfunctioning part in accordance with further inspection result. {: #wp1060126}

2 {: #wp1060130}

INSPECT TO SEE WHETHER MALFUNCTION IS IN VENT MODE OR OTHER MODES {: #wp1060133} • Does air blow out when in VENT mode? {: #wp1060258}

Yes {: #wp1060136}

Go to Step 5. {: #wp1060139}

No {: #wp1060149}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060152}

3 {: #wp1060156}

INSPECT VENT {: #wp1060159} • Is vent clogged? {: #wp1060319}

Yes {: #wp1060162}

Remove obstruction, then go to Step 9. {: #wp1060165}

No {: #wp1060296}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060299}

4 {: #wp1060351}

VERIFY THAT DUCT IN DASHBOARD IS INSTALLED {: #wp1060354} • Is duct in dashboard properly installed? {: #wp1060393}

Yes {: #wp1060357}

Inspect duct for clogging, deformity and air leakage, then go to Step 9. {: #wp1060360}

No {: #wp1060370}

Install duct securely in the proper position, then go to Step 9. {: #wp1060373}

5 {: #wp1060425}

INSPECT TO SEE WHETHER MALFUNCTION IS IN HEAT MODE OR DEFROSTER MODE {: #wp1060428} • Does air blow out when in HEAT mode? {: #wp1060467}

Yes {: #wp1060431}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060434}

No {: #wp1060444}

Inspect vent for clogging, then go to Step 9. {: #wp1060447}

6 {: #wp1060499}

INSPECT DEFROSTER MODE {: #wp1060502} • Does air blow out when in DEFROSTER mode? {: #wp1060541}

Yes {: #wp1060505}

Operation is okay. Recheck malfunction symptoms. {: #wp1060508}

No {: #wp1060518}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060521}

7 {: #wp1060573}

INSPECT VENT {: #wp1060576} • Is vent clogged? {: #wp1060615}

Yes {: #wp1060579}

Remove obstruction, then go to Step 9. {: #wp1060582}

No {: #wp1060592}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060595}

8 {: #wp1060647}

VERIFY THAT DEFROSTER DUCT IS INSTALLED {: #wp1060650} • Is defroster duct properly installed? {: #wp1060689}

Yes {: #wp1060653}

Inspect duct for clogging, deformity, and air leakage, then go to the next step. {: #wp1060656}

No {: #wp1060666}

Install duct securely in proper position, then go to the next step. {: #wp1060669}

9 {: #wp1060721}

CONFIRM THAT MALFUNCTION SYMPTOMS DO NOT RECUR AFTER REPAIR {: #wp1060724} • Does air blow out? {: #wp1060763}

Yes {: #wp1060727}

Troubleshooting completed. {: #wp1060730} Explain repairs to customer. {: #wp1060787}

No {: #wp1060740}

Recheck malfunction symptoms, then repeat from Step 1 if malfunction recurs. {: #wp1060743}
