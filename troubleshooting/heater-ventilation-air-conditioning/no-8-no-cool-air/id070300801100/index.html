• Magnetic clutch does not operate. {: #wp1059857}

• Malfunction in PCM A/C cut control system {: #wp1059864} • Malfunction in climate control unit {: #wp1059934} • Malfunction in refrigerant pressure switch {: #wp1059944} • Malfunction in PCM (A/C signal) {: #wp1059954} • Malfunction in PCM (IG1 signal) {: #wp1059966} • Malfunction in A/C compressor {: #wp1059976} • Malfunction in A/C relay {: #wp1059988} • Malfunction in evaporator temperature sensor {: #wp1060000}

• When performing an asterisked (*) troubleshooting inspection, shake the wiring harness and connectors while performing the inspection to discover whether poor contact points are the cause of any intermittent malfunctions. If there is a problem, check to make sure connectors, terminals and wiring harnesses are connected correctly and undamaged.
