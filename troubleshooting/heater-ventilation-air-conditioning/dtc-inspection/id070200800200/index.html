DTC INSPECTION

id070200800200

{: #wp1059798}

  1. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position, then press and hold the A/C switch for 5 s or more within 10 s.

  2. Turn the fan switch to the OFF position.

  3. Verify that all the indicator lights of the climate control unit illuminate.{: #wp1059817}

  1. Press the recirculate switch or turn the ignition switch off to finish.

Malfunction detection function

  1. Shine a 60 W incandescent light from a distance of approx. 100 mm vertically to the solar radiation sensor.

*Note*{: #wp1059893}

•  If incandescent light is not shone on the solar radiation sensor, the climate control unit determines that there is a malfunction

  1. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position, then press and hold the A/C switch for 5 s or more within 10 s.

  2. Turn the fan switch to the AUTO position.

  3. Turn and adjust the mode dial to the inspection item.

•  If the system is normal, the A/C switch indicator light illuminates.

Door position check function

  1. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position, then press and hold the A/C switch for 5 s or more within 10 s.

  2. Turn the fan switch to the LO position.

  3. Verify that the indicator light of the climate control unit illuminates.

Operation check function

  1. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position, then press and hold the A/C switch for 5 s or more within 10 s.

  2. Turn the fan switch to a position other than OFF, AUTO, or LO positions.

  3. Verify the operation of each output related part by switching the mode dial positions while referring to the operation check table.

• If there is any malfunction, inspect the malfunctioning system.
