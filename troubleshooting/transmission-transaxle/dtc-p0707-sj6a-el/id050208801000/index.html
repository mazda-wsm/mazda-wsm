Transmission range (TR) switch circuit low input (short to ground) {: #wp1059844}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059784}

• TR switch position voltage input to TCM is less than 0.127 V when ignition switch is at ON position. {: #wp1059786} Diagnostic support note:} • The AT warning light illuminates if the TCM detects the above malfunction condition during the first drive cycle. {: #wp1059926} • A PENDING CODE is not available. {: #wp1059936} • FREEZE FRAME DATA is available. {: #wp1059946} • MIL does not illuminate. {: #wp1059956} • The DTC is stored in the TCM memory. {: #wp1059966

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059859}

• TR switch malfunction {: #wp1059862} • TR switch misadjustment {: #wp1059998} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TR switch terminal B and TCM terminal 2O {: #wp1060008} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TR switch terminal C and TCM terminal 2M {: #wp1060018} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TR switch terminal H and TCM terminal 2N {: #wp1060028} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TR switch terminal D and TCM terminal 2K {: #wp1060038} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TR switch terminal B and TCM terminal 2O {: #wp1060048} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TR switch terminal C and TCM terminal 2M {: #wp1060058} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TR switch terminal H and TCM terminal 2N {: #wp1060068} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TR switch terminal D and TCM terminal 2K {: #wp1060078} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TR switch terminal E and ignition switch (IG1) {: #wp1060088} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TR switch terminal E and ignition switch (IG1) {: #wp1060098} • Damaged connector between TR switch and TCM {: #wp1060108} • TCM malfunction {: #wp1060118}
