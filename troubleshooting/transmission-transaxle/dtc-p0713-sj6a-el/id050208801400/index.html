DTC P0713 [SJ6A-EL]

id050208801400

{: #wp1059781}

DTC P0713 }{: #wp1061567

Transmission fluid temperature (TFT) sensor circuit malfunction (short to power/open circuit) {: #wp1059844}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059784}

• If the TCM detects the following condition for 1 s or more, the TCM determines that the TFT sensor circuit has a malfunction. {: #wp1059786} - Engine warmed-up {: #wp1059918}- ATF temperature less than -43 °C {-45.4 °F} {: #wp1059933} Diagnostic support note:} • The MIL illuminates if the TCM detects the above malfunction condition during first drive cycle. {: #wp1059970} • A PENDING CODE is not available. {: #wp1059980} • FREEZE FRAME DATA is available. {: #wp1059990} • The AT warning light illuminates. {: #wp1060000} • The DTC is stored in the TCM memory. {: #wp1060010

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059859}

• TFT sensor malfunction {: #wp1059862} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TFT sensor and TCM terminal 1J {: #wp1060042} • Open circuit in wiring harness between TFT sensor and TCM terminal 1M {: #wp1060052} • Short to power in wiring harness between TFT sensor and TCM terminal 1J {: #wp1060062} • Short to power in wiring harness between TFT sensor and TCM terminal 1M {: #wp1060072} • Damaged connector between TFT sensor and TCM {: #wp1060082} • TCM malfunction {: #wp1060092}
