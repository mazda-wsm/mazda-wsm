DTC P2764 }{: #wp1062217

TCC control solenoid circuit malfunction (short to ground/open circuit) {: #wp1059844}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059784}

• Open or short circuit in TCC control solenoid signal system (while TCM monitors solenoid output voltage, the voltage that differs from the signal output by CPU in TCM is detected). {: #wp1059786} Diagnostic support note:} • The MIL illuminates if the TCM detects the above malfunction condition during the first drive cycle. {: #wp1059919} • A PENDING CODE is not available. {: #wp1059929} • FREEZE FRAME DATA is available. {: #wp1059939} • The AT warning light illuminates. {: #wp1059949} • The DTC is stored in the TCM memory. {: #wp1059959

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059859}

• Open circuit between TCC control solenoid terminal B and TCM terminal 1Q {: #wp1059862} • Open circuit between TCC control solenoid terminal A and TCM terminal 1D {: #wp1059991} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TCC control solenoid terminal B and TCM terminal 1Q {: #wp1060001} • Short to ground in wiring harness between TCC control solenoid terminal A and TCM terminal 1D {: #wp1060011} • TCC control solenoid malfunction {: #wp1060021} • Damaged connector between TCC control solenoid and TCM {: #wp1060031} • TCM malfunction {: #wp1060041}
