DTC P0826 [SJ6A-EL]

id050208811400

{: #wp1059780}

DTC P0826 }{: #wp1061676

Steering shift switch circuit malfunction (open circuit/short to ground) {: #wp1059843}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059783}

• TCM detects short circuit or short to ground in steering shift switch circuit when engine is running. {: #wp1059785} Diagnostic support note:} • MIL illuminates if TCM detects the above malfunction condition during the first drive cycle. {: #wp1059918} • PENDING CODE is not available. {: #wp1059928} • FREEZE FRAME DATA is available. {: #wp1059938} • AT warning light illuminate. {: #wp1059948} • DTC is stored in the TCM memory. {: #wp1059958

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059858}

• Steering shift switch malfunction {: #wp1059861} • Open circuit in wiring harness between steering shift switch terminal 2F and TCM terminal 2AF {: #wp1059990} • Short to ground in wiring harness between steering shift switch terminal 2F and TCM terminal 2AF {: #wp1060000} • Open circuit in wiring harness between steering shift switch terminal 2C and TCM terminal 2AB {: #wp1060010} • Damaged connector between steering shift switch and TCM {: #wp1060020} • Clock spring malfunction {: #wp1060030} • TCM malfunction {: #wp1060040}
