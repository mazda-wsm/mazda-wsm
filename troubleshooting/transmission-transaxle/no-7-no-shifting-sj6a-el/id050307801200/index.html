NO.7 NO SHIFTING [SJ6A-EL]

id050307801200

{: #wp1059780}

7 }{: #wp1061255

No shifting {: #wp1059785}

DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059858}

• Single shift range only. {: #wp1059861} • Sometimes it shifts correctly. {: #wp1059914}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059865}

• When the gear position is fixed in 4GR or 5GR due to the fail-safe operation, malfunction is in the AT. {: #wp1059868} - Clutch burnt (C1 clutch, C2 clutch, C3 clutch, B1 brake, B2 brake, B3 brake, B4 brake) {: #wp1059947} • Line pressure low {: #wp1059961}• Incorrect throttle position signal {: #wp1059975}• Incorrect accelerator pedal position signal {: #wp1059985}• Incorrect engine speed signal {: #wp1059995}• Incorrect engine coolant temperature signal {: #wp1060005}• Incorrect engine torque signal {: #wp1060015}• VSS malfunction {: #wp1060025}• Turbine sensor malfunction {: #wp1060035}• Sensor GND malfunction {: #wp1060045}• Shift solenoid A malfunction {: #wp1060055}• Shift solenoid B malfunction {: #wp1060065}• Shift solenoid C malfunction {: #wp1060075}• Shift solenoid D malfunction {: #wp1060085}• Shift solenoid E malfunction {: #wp1060095}• Line pressure control solenoid malfunction {: #wp1060105}• Shift solenoid F malfunction {: #wp1060115}• Shift solenoid G malfunction {: #wp1060125}• Control valve body malfunction {: #wp1060135} - 4GR or 5GR is fixed (Operation in fail-safe function) {: #wp1060145} • Short or open circuit in wiring harness {: #wp1060156}• Poor connection of connector {: #wp1060170}• Poor ground of shift solenoid {: #wp1060180}• Malfunction of electronic parts of output and input system {: #wp1060190} - TFT sensor malfunction {: #wp1060200}- TR switch malfunction {: #wp1060210}- TCM malfunction {: #wp1060220}

Diagnostic procedure

STEP {: #wp1060344}

INSPECTION {: #wp1060346}

ACTION {: #wp1060350}

1 {: #wp1060238}

• With the ignition switch at the ON position, does the gear position indicator light indication corresponded to the selector lever position? {: #wp1060240}

Yes {: #wp1060296}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060299}

No {: #wp1060379}

Go to No.27 "GEAR POSITION INDICATOR LIGHT DOES NOT ILLUMINATE IN M RANGE", or No.28 "GEAR POSITION INDICATOR LIGHT ILLUMINATES IN D RANGE OR P, N, R POSITIONS". {: #wp1060382}

2 {: #wp1060386}

• Access the following PCM PIDs using the M-MDS. {: #wp1060389} - RPM {: #wp1060507}- APP {: #wp1060521}- TP {: #wp1060531}- ECT {: #wp1060541}- ENG_TRQ {: #wp1060551} • Are PIDs value normal? {: #wp1060563}

Yes {: #wp1060392}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060395}

No {: #wp1060405}

Inspect and repair suspected PID related part. {: #wp1060408}

3 {: #wp1060412}

• Inspect the TFT sensor. {: #wp1060415} (See TRANSMISSION FLUID TEMPERATURE (TFT) SENSOR INSPECTION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060624} • Is the TFT sensor normal? {: #wp1060638}

Yes {: #wp1060418}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060421}

No {: #wp1060597}

Replace the TFT sensor. {: #wp1060600}

4 {: #wp1060666}

• Stop the engine. {: #wp1060669} • Inspect following solenoid. {: #wp1060720} (See SOLENOID VALVE INSPECTION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060730} - Shift solenoid A {: #wp1060744}- Shift solenoid B {: #wp1060758}- Shift solenoid C {: #wp1060768}- Shift solenoid D {: #wp1060778}- Shift solenoid E {: #wp1060788}- Line pressure control solenoid {: #wp1060798}- Shift solenoid F {: #wp1060808}- Shift solenoid G {: #wp1060818} • Are they normal? {: #wp1060828}

Yes {: #wp1060672}

Inspect the ATF condition. {: #wp1060675} • If a large amount of metal specks are found, replace the transmission. {: #wp1060852} (See AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060866} • If a large amount of metal specks are not found, replace the control valve body. {: #wp1060880} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY REMOVAL [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060890} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY INSTALLATION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060904}

No {: #wp1060687}

Replace the control valve body. {: #wp1060690} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY REMOVAL [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060928} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY INSTALLATION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060942}

5 {: #wp1060954}

• Verify test results. {: #wp1060957} - If normal, return to the diagnostic index to service any additional symptoms. {: #wp1061015}- If malfunction remains, inspect the related Service Information and perform repair or diagnosis. {: #wp1061029} • If the vehicle is repaired, troubleshooting completed. {: #wp1061039}• If the vehicle is not repaired or additional diagnostic information is not available, replace the TCM. {: #wp1061053}
