NO.22 NO ENGINE BRAKING [SJ6A-EL]

id050307809600

{: #wp1059780}

22 }{: #wp1060897

No engine braking {: #wp1059785}

DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059858}

• Engine speed drops to idle but the vehicle coasts when the accelerator pedal is released during cruising at medium to high speeds. {: #wp1059861} • Engine speed drops to idle but the vehicle coasts when accelerator pedal is released when in M range (1GR) at low vehicle speed. {: #wp1059914}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059865}

• Clutch slippage, burnt (C4 clutch/B1 brake/B2 brake/B4 brake) {: #wp1059868} - Line pressure low {: #wp1059952} • VSS malfunction {: #wp1059966}• Improper signal from PCM (engine speed, accelerator pedal position, throttle position, engine coolant temperature, engine torque signals) {: #wp1059980}• Line pressure control solenoid malfunction {: #wp1059990}• Control valve body malfunction {: #wp1060000} *Note*{: #wp1060010} • Before following the troubleshooting steps, make sure that the Automatic Transmission On-Board Diagnostic and Automatic Transmission Basic Inspection are conducted. (See BASIC INSPECTION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060820}

Diagnostic procedure

STEP {: #wp1060155}

INSPECTION {: #wp1060157}

ACTION {: #wp1060161}

1 {: #wp1060049}

• Do the following symptoms occur concurrently? {: #wp1060051} - Engine flares up or slips during acceleration {: #wp1060271}- Engine flares up or slips when shifting {: #wp1060285}

Yes {: #wp1060107}

Go to No.14 "ENGINE FLARES UP OR SLIPS WHEN UPSHIFTING OR DOWNSHIFTING", or No.15 "ENGINE FLARES UP OR SLIPS WHEN ACCELERATING VEHICLE". {: #wp1060110}

No {: #wp1060190}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060193}

2 {: #wp1060197}

• Access the following PCM PIDs using the M-MDS. {: #wp1060200} - RPM {: #wp1060342}- APP {: #wp1060356}- TP {: #wp1060366}- ECT {: #wp1060376}- ENG_TRQ {: #wp1060386} • Are PIDs value normal? {: #wp1060398}

Yes {: #wp1060203}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060206}

No {: #wp1060216}

Inspect and repair suspected PID related part. {: #wp1060219}

3 {: #wp1060223}

• Stop the engine. {: #wp1060226} • Inspect line pressure control solenoid. {: #wp1060459} (See SOLENOID VALVE INSPECTION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060469} • Is the normal? {: #wp1060483}

Yes {: #wp1060229}

Inspect the ATF condition. {: #wp1060232} • If a large amount of metal specks are found, replace the transmission. {: #wp1060503} (See AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060517} • If a large amount of metal specks are not found, replace the control valve body. {: #wp1060531} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY REMOVAL [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060541} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY INSTALLATION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060555}

No {: #wp1060432}

Replace the control valve body. {: #wp1060435} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY REMOVAL [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060579} (See CONTROL VALVE BODY INSTALLATION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060593}

4 {: #wp1060605}

• Verify test results. {: #wp1060608} - If normal, return to the diagnostic index to service any additional symptoms. {: #wp1060662}- If malfunction remains, inspect the related Service Information and perform repair or diagnosis. {: #wp1060676} • If the vehicle is repaired, troubleshooting completed. {: #wp1060686}• If the vehicle is not repaired or additional diagnostic information is not available, replace the TCM. {: #wp1060700}
