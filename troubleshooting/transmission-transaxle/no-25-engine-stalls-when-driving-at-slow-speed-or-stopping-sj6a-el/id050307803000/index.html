NO.25 ENGINE STALLS WHEN DRIVING AT SLOW SPEED OR STOPPING [SJ6A-EL]

id050307803000

{: #wp1059780}

25 }{: #wp1060500

Engine stalls when driving at slow speeds or stopping {: #wp1059785}

DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059858}

• Engine stalls when the brake pedal is depressed while driving at low speed or stopping. {: #wp1059861}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059865}

• The malfunction is in the engine control system (e.g. Fuel injection control, electronic controlled throttle system) {: #wp1059868} *Note*{: #wp1059936} • Before following the troubleshooting steps, make sure that the Automatic Transmission On-Board Diagnostic and Automatic Transmission Basic Inspection are conducted. (See BASIC INSPECTION [SJ6A-EL].) {: #wp1060423}

Diagnostic procedure

STEP {: #wp1060081}

INSPECTION {: #wp1060083}

ACTION {: #wp1060087}

1 {: #wp1059975}

• Go to symptom troubleshooting No.10 "LOW IDLE/STALLS DURING DECELERATION". {: #wp1059977} (See NO.10 LOW IDLE/STALLS DURING DECELERATION [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060203} • Is the engine control system normal? {: #wp1060217}

Yes {: #wp1060033}

Go to No.24 "ENGINE STALLS WHEN SHIFTED TO D RANGE, OR IN R POSITION". {: #wp1060036}

No {: #wp1060116}

Repair or replace any malfunctioning parts according to the inspection results. {: #wp1060119}
