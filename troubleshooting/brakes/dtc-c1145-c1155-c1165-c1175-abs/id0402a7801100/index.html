DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059917}

• Open circuit or short to ground has been detected in the ABS wheel-speed sensor wiring harness on any of the four vehicle wheels. {: #wp1059923}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059930}

• Open circuit or short to ground in the wiring harness between the following ABS HU/CM terminal and the ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal: {: #wp1059936} - ABS HU/CM terminal M-RF ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal A {: #wp1060110}- ABS HU/CM terminal O-RF ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal B {: #wp1060129}- ABS HU/CM terminal F-LF ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal A {: #wp1060144}- ABS HU/CM terminal E-LF ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal B {: #wp1060159}- ABS HU/CM terminal I-RR ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal A {: #wp1060174}- ABS HU/CM terminal L-RR ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal B {: #wp1060189}- ABS HU/CM terminal G-LR ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal A {: #wp1060204}- ABS HU/CM terminal H-LR ABS wheel-speed sensor terminal B {: #wp1060219} • ABS wheel-speed sensor malfunction {: #wp1060234} • Poor connection at connectors (female terminal) {: #wp1060245}
