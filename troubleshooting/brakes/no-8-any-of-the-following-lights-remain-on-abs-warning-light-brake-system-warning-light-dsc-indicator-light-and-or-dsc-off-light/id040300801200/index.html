NO.8 ANY OF THE FOLLOWING LIGHTS REMAIN ON: (ABS WARNING LIGHT, BRAKE SYSTEM WARNING LIGHT, DSC INDICATOR LIGHT AND/OR DSC OFF LIGHT)

id040300801200

{: #wp1059778}

8 }{: #wp1061136

Any of the following lights remain on: (ABS warning light, BRAKE system warning light, DSC indicator light and/or DSC OFF light) {: #wp1059783}

[TROUBLESHOOTING HINTS] {: #wp1059856} • Brake fluid amount is low {: #wp1059907} • Parking brake does not release {: #wp1059925} • No connection at DSC HU/CM connector {: #wp1059939} (When DSC HU/CM connector is disconnected, ABS warning light and BRAKE system warning light illuminate) {: #wp1059955} • DSC HU/CM detected malfunction (Input and output device malfunction) {: #wp1059967} • DSC HU/CM detects low voltage in power supply {: #wp1059983} • DSC HU/CM ground malfunction {: #wp1059999} (When DSC HU/CM ground is not securely connected, ABS warning light and BRAKE system warning light illuminate but diagnostic trouble code does not displayed) {: #wp1060015} • DSC HU/CM does not operate (DSC HU/CM malfunction) {: #wp1060027}

Diagnostic procedure

STEP {: #wp1060162}

INSPECTION {: #wp1060164}

ACTION {: #wp1060168}

1 {: #wp1060056}

INSPECT BRAKE FLUID AMOUNT AND VERIFY THAT PARKING BRAKE RELEASES} • Is the brake fluid amount normal? {: #wp1060270} • Is the parking brake lever released? {: #wp1060284

Yes {: #wp1060114}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060117}

No {: #wp1060197}

Add brake fluid or release parking brake lever. {: #wp1060200}

2 {: #wp1060204}

INSPECT DTCS IN DSC HU/CM} • Inspect the DTC for the DSC ON-BOARD DIAGNOSTIC SYSTEM. {: #wp1060326} • Have DTCs been stored in memory? {: #wp1060341

Yes {: #wp1060210}

Perform inspection using appropriate DTC. {: #wp1060213} (See DTC Table.) {: #wp1060361}

No {: #wp1060223}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060226}

3 {: #wp1060230}

INSPECT WHETHER MALFUNCTION IS IN CONTROL MODULE CONNECTOR, TERMINAL OR OTHER • Do the ABS warning light and BRAKE system warning light go out after 4 s when the ignition switch is turned to the ON position? {: #wp1060414}

Yes {: #wp1060236}

Temporary poor connection in control module connector. {: #wp1060239} Inspect DSC HU/CM connector, then go to Step 6. {: #wp1060447} Inspect DSC HU/CM connector terminal, then go to Step 7. {: #wp1060455}

No {: #wp1060389}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060392}

4 {: #wp1060471}

INSPECT BATTERY} • Is the battery voltage normal? {: #wp1060525

Yes {: #wp1060477}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060480}

No {: #wp1060494}

Inspect the battery and charging system. {: #wp1060497} (See BATTERY INSPECTION [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060559} (See GENERATOR INSPECTION [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060573}

5 {: #wp1060585}

INSPECT CHARGING SYSTEM} • Is the battery voltage normal with electrical load (A/C, headlight, etc.) on and engine idling? {: #wp1060655

Yes {: #wp1060591}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060594}

No {: #wp1060612}

Inspect the charging system (drive belt tension, generator, etc.). {: #wp1060615} (See GENERATOR INSPECTION [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060691}

6 {: #wp1060703}

VERIFY THAT DSC HU/CM CONNECTOR IS CONNECTED} • Is the DSC HU/CM securely connected? {: #wp1060783

Yes {: #wp1060709}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060712}

No {: #wp1060732}

Connect the DSC HU/CM connector securely, then go to the next step. {: #wp1060735}

7 {: #wp1060819}

VERIFY THAT DSC HU/CM CONNECTOR TERMINAL OR RELATED CONNECTOR TERMINALS ARE CONNECTED} • Are the DSC HU/CM connector terminal or instrument cluster connector terminal etc. related connector terminals securely connected? {: #wp1060899

Yes {: #wp1060825}

Replace the DSC HU/CM. {: #wp1060828} (See DSC HU/CM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060927}

No {: #wp1060848}

Securely connect the DSC HU/CM connector terminal and related connector terminals. {: #wp1060851}
