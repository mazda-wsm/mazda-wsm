C1279, C1280, C1281, C1282, C1951, C1952, C1959, C2768 {: #wp1059784}

Combined sensor system {: #wp1059843}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059917}

• C1279 {: #wp1059923} - The yaw rate value from the combined sensor (yaw rate part) exceeds specification while the vehicle is stopped or while accelerating from a standstill. {: #wp1060020} • C1280 {: #wp1060034} - The difference between the yaw rate value calculated by each sensor and the yaw rate value from the combined sensor (yaw rate part) exceeds specification. {: #wp1060044} • C1281 {: #wp1060058} - The difference between the lateral-G value calculated by each sensor and the lateral-G value from the combined sensor (lateral-G part) exceeds specification. {: #wp1060068} • C1282 {: #wp1060085} - Specified signal pattern from combined sensor has not output after the ignition switch is turned to the ON position. {: #wp1060095} • C1951 {: #wp1060109} - The monitor voltage from the combined sensor (lateral-G part) is not within specification. {: #wp1060119} • C1952 {: #wp1060134} - The monitor voltage from the combined sensor (yaw rate part) is not within specification. {: #wp1060144} • C1959 {: #wp1060158} - The lateral-G value from the combined sensor (lateral-G part) exceeds specification. {: #wp1060168} • C2768 {: #wp1060184} - There is an abnormality in the signal from the combined sensor (yaw rate part). {: #wp1060194}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059930}

• Open circuit in the wiring harness between ignition switch and combined sensor terminal E or short circuit to ground {: #wp1059936} • Open circuit in the wiring harness between DSC HU/CM terminal Y and combined sensor terminal F or short circuit to ground {: #wp1060230} • Open circuit in the wiring harness between DSC HU/CM terminal AB and combined sensor terminal C or short circuit to ground {: #wp1060242} • Open circuit in the wiring harness between DSC HU/CM terminal AE and combined sensor terminal A or short circuit to ground {: #wp1060254} • Open circuit in the wiring harness between DSC HU/CM terminal AA and combined sensor terminal B or short circuit to ground {: #wp1060266} • Open circuit in the wiring harness between DSC HU/CM terminal AI and combined sensor terminal D {: #wp1060278} • Combined sensor malfunction {: #wp1060290} • Poor connection at connectors (female terminal) {: #wp1060300}
