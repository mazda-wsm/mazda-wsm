DTC B1317, B1318 [DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL]

id0402b2800300

{: #wp1059779}

DTC }{: #wp1061594

B1317, B1318 {: #wp1059784}

Power supply system {: #wp1059843}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059917}

• B1317 {: #wp1059923} - The voltage at DSC HU/CM terminal J is 16.8 V or more. {: #wp1060020} • B1318 {: #wp1060045} - The vehicle speed exceeds 6 km/h and the voltage at ABS HU/CM terminal J is less than 9.6 V {: #wp1060087}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059930}

• ENGINE 15 A fuse malfunction {: #wp1059936} • Open circuit or short to ground in the wiring harness between the DSC HU/CM terminal J and the battery {: #wp1060123} • Open circuit or faulty ground in the wiring harness between the DSC HU/CM terminal D and the body ground {: #wp1060135} • Battery deterioration {: #wp1060147} • Generator malfunction {: #wp1060157} • Poor connection at connectors (female terminal) {: #wp1060167}
