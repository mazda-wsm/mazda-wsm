AM/FM ANTENNA FEEDER NO.1 REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id092000814000

{: #wp1059780}

  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.

  2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Console (See CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059800}(2) Glove compartment (See GLOVE COMPARTMENT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059818}(3) Side wall (See SIDE WALL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059836}(4) Console panel (See CONSOLE PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059854}(5) Center panel unit (See CENTER PANEL UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059872}(6) Lower panel (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059890}(7) Column cover (See COLUMN COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059908}(8) Driver-side air bag module (See DRIVER-SIDE AIR BAG MODULE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059926}(9) Steering wheel (See STEERING WHEEL AND COLUMN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059945}(10) Combination switch (See COMBINATION SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059963}(11) Steering shaft (See STEERING WHEEL AND COLUMN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059981}(12) Meter hood (See METER HOOD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059999}(13) Instrument cluster (See INSTRUMENT CLUSTER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060017}(14) Side panel (See SIDE PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060035}(15) Bonnet release lever (See BONNET LATCH AND RELEASE LEVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060053}(16) Female wedge (See CONVERTIBLE TOP DISASSEMBLY/ASSEMBLY.) {: #wp1060071}(17) A-pillar trim (See A-PILLAR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060089}(18) Scuff plate (See SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060108}(19) Front side trim (See FRONT SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060126}(20) Fuse box No.1 {: #wp1060144}(21) Dashboard (See DASHBOARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  1. Remove clips A and B.

  2. Disconnect the connector.

  3. Remove AM/FM antenna feeder No.1.

  4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
