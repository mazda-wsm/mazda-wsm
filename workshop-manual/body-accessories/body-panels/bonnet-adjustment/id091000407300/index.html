BONNET ADJUSTMENT

id091000407300

{: #wp1059776}

Gap Adjustment

  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (SeeBATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

  2. Remove the following parts:

(1) Front bumper (See FRONT BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059814}(2) Front combination lights (See FRONT COMBINATION LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059832}(3) Side step molding (SeeSIDE STEP MOLDING REMOVAL.) (SeeSIDE STEP MOLDING INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059869}(4) Front fender panel (See FRONT FENDER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  1. Loosen the bonnet hinge installation bolts and adjust the bonnet.{: #wp1059895}

  1. Tighten the bolts.

  2. Verify that the gap between the bonnet and the body is within the specification.{: #wp1059932}

Standard clearancea: 3.5-6.5 mm {0.14-0.25 in}b: 3.0-5.0 mm {0.12-0.19 in}

Height Difference Adjustment

  1. Turn the stop rubber to adjust the height of the bonnet.{: #wp1060017}

  1. Verify that the height difference between the bonnet and the body is within the specification.{: #wp1060044}

Standard clearancea: -1.0-2.0 mm {-0.04-0.07 in}b: -1.5-1.5 mm {-0.06-0.05 in}b: -0.5-1.5 mm {-0.02-0.05 in}
