REAR SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

id091700806000

{: #wp1059778}

Except Power Retractable Hardtop

  1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Console (See CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059798}(2) Quarter trim (See QUARTER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059816}(3) Scuff plate (SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059834}(4) Tire house trim (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059852}(5) Aeroboard (See AEROBOARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059870}(6) Seat back bar garnish (front) (See SEAT BACK BAR GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059888}(7) Back trim (See BACK TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059906}(8) Seat belt set plate (See SEAT BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1059924}(9) Seat back crossmember assembly (See SEAT BACK CROSSMEMBER ASSEMBLY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  1. Remove the fasteners.{: #wp1059960}

  1. Pull the rear side trim outward and detach pins A, B and tab C.

  2. Remove the rear side trim.

  3. Install in the reverse order of removal.

Power Retractable Hardtop

  1. Remove the following parts:

(1) Console (See CONSOLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060037}(2) Quarter trim (See QUARTER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060055}(3) Scuff plate (SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060073}(4) Tire house trim (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060091}(5) Aeroboard (See AEROBOARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060109}(6) Front seat back bar garnish (See SEAT BACK BAR GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.) {: #wp1060127}(7) Back trim (See BACK TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  1. Disconnect the power retractable hardtop drain hose. (See POWER RETRACTABLE HARDTOP DRAIN HOSE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  2. Remove the fasteners.{: #wp1060181}

  1. Remove the clip A and pin.

  2. Remove the rear side trim.

  3. Install the reverse order of removal.
