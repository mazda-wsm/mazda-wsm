POWER WINDOW MAIN SWITCH INSPECTION [EXCEPT POWER RETRACTABLE HARDTOP]

id091200419000

{: #wp1059778}

Auto Open Function For Driver's Side (L.H.D.)

Driver's side

  1. Measure the voltage at each terminal (other than terminal J).

• If the voltage is not as specified in the Terminal Voltage Table, inspect the parts under "Inspection item (s)" and related wiring harnesses.

  1. Remove the battery cover.

  2. Disconnect the negative battery cable.(See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

  3. Inspect the power window main switch connector for continuity at terminal J.

  4. If the system does not work properly even though the inspection items or related wiring harnesses do not have any malfunction, replace the power window main switch.

  1. Verify that the continuity between the power window main switch terminals is as indicated in the table.{: #wp1060494}
