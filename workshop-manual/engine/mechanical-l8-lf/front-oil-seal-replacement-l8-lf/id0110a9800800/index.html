Crankshaft Pulley Lock Bolt Removal Note

  1. Remove the cylinder block lower blind plug.

  2. Install the SST.{: #wp1060152}

  1. Turn the crankshaft clockwise until the crankshaft is in the No.1 cylinder TDC position (until the balance weight contacts the SST).

  2. Hold the crankshaft pulley using the SSTs.{: #wp1060207}

  1. Remove the crankshaft pulley lock bolt.

Front Oil Seal Removal Note

  1. Cut the oil seal lip using a razor knife.

  2. Remove the oil seal using a screwdriver wrapped with a rag.{: #wp1060273}

Front Oil Seal Installation Note

  1. Apply clean engine oil to a new oil seal.

  2. Push the front oil seal in the engine front cover by hand.

  3. Tap the oil seal in evenly using the SST and a hammer.{: #wp1060330}

Front oil seal press-in amount0-1.0 mm {0-0.039 in}
