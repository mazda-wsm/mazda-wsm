CYLINDER HEAD GASKET REPLACEMENT [L8, LF]

id0110a9800700

Warning•  Fuel vapor is hazardous. It can very easily ignite, causing serious injury and damage. Always keep sparks and flames away from fuel.

•  Fuel line spills and leakage are dangerous. Fuel can ignite and cause serious injuries or death and damage. Fuel can also irritate skin and eyes. To prevent this, always complete the “Fuel Line Safety Procedure”. (See BEFORE SERVICE PRECAUTION [L8, LF].)

1.  Perform “Fuel Line Safety Procedures“. Leave the fuel pump relay removed. (See BEFORE SERVICE PRECAUTION [L8, LF].)

2.  Remove the battery and battery tray. (See BATTERY REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

3.  Drain the engine coolant. (See ENGINE COOLANT REPLACEMENT [L8, LF].)

4.  Remove the front suspension tower bar (joint, right side, left side). (See FRONT SUSPENSION TOWER BAR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

5.  Remove the air cleaner. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

6.  Remove the dynamic chamber. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

7.  Remove the ignition coil. (See IGNITION COIL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

8.  Remove the drive belt. (See TIMING CHAIN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

9.  Remove the CKP sensor. (See CRANKSHAFT POSITION (CKP) SENSOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

10.  Remove the P/S oil pump with the oil hose still connected and position the P/S oil pump so that it is out of the way. (See POWER STEERING OIL PUMP REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

11.  Remove the timing chain. (See TIMING CHAIN REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

12.  Remove the wiper arm. (See WIPER ARM AND BLADE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

13.  Remove the cowl grille. (See COWL GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

14.  Remove the side cowl grille. (See SIDE COWL GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

15.  Remove the service hole cover. (See EGR VALVE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

16.  Disconnect the generator, but do not remove it from the vehicle. Fix the generator using a rope to prevent it from falling. (See GENERATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

17.  Remove the exhaust manifold. (See EXHAUST SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [L8, LF].)

18.  Remove in the order indicated in the table.

19.  Install in the reverse order of removal.

20.  Inspect the compression. (See COMPRESSION INSPECTION [L8, LF].)

21.  Start the engine and:

•  Inspect for the engine coolant leakage.

•  Verify the ignition timing, idle speed and idle mixture. (See ENGINE TUNE-UP [L8, LF].)
