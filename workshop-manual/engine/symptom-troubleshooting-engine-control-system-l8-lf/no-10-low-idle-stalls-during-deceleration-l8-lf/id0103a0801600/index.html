NO.10 LOW IDLE/STALLS DURING DECELERATION [L8, LF]

id0103a0801600

{: #wp1059781}

10 }{: #wp1061648

LOW IDLE/STALLS DURING DECELERATION {: #wp1059844}

DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059784}

• Engine stops unexpectedly at the beginning of deceleration or recovery from deceleration. {: #wp1059786}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059859}

• Vacuum leakage {: #wp1059862} • Improper operation of electronic throttle control system {: #wp1059931} • Air leakage from intake-air system {: #wp1059941} • Improper air/fuel mixture ratio control {: #wp1059952} • Evaporative emission control system malfunction {: #wp1059964} • Accelerator pedal position sensor or related circuit malfunction {: #wp1059974} • TP sensor or related circuit malfunction {: #wp1059984} • MAF sensor or related circuit malfunction {: #wp1059994} • Brake switch or related circuit malfunction {: #wp1060004} • Neutral/clutch pedal position switch or related circuit malfunction (MT) {: #wp1060014} • TR switch or related circuit malfunction (AT) {: #wp1060026} • Improper A/C magnetic clutch operation {: #wp1060036}

INSPECTION {: #wp1060164}

RESULTS {: #wp1060166}

ACTION {: #wp1060168}

1 {: #wp1060056}

Does the engine idle rough? {: #wp1060058}

Yes {: #wp1060114}

Go to symptom troubleshooting "No.8 Engine runs rough/rolling idle". {: #wp1060117} (See NO.8 ENGINE RUNS ROUGH/ROLLING IDLE [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060291}

No {: #wp1060197}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060200}

2 {: #wp1060204}

Turn off the A/C switch and fan switch. {: #wp1060207} Does the A/C magnetic clutch engage? {: #wp1060331}

No {: #wp1060223}

3 {: #wp1060230}

Go to the next step. {: #wp1060239}

No {: #wp1060384}

No {: #wp1060616}

Repair or replace malfunctioning part according to inspection results. {: #wp1060619}

6 {: #wp1060691}

Inspect the evaporative emission control system. {: #wp1060700}

No {: #wp1060718}
