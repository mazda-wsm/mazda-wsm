POOR FUEL ECONOMY {: #wp1059842}

DESCRIPTION {: #wp1059782}

Fuel economy is unsatisfactory. {: #wp1059784}

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059857}

• Contaminated air cleaner element {: #wp1059860} • Variable intake-air control malfunction {: #wp1059919} • Engine cooling system malfunction {: #wp1059930} • Weak spark {: #wp1059940} • Poor fuel quality {: #wp1059950} • Erratic or no signal from CMP sensor {: #wp1059960} • Clutch slippage (MT) {: #wp1059970} • Improper ATF level (AT) {: #wp1059980} • Variable tumble control malfunction {: #wp1059990} • Improper operation of variable valve timing control system {: #wp1060000} • Improper coolant level {: #wp1060010} • Inadequate fuel pressure {: #wp1060020} • Spark plug malfunction {: #wp1060030} • PCV valve malfunction {: #wp1060040} • Brake dragging {: #wp1060050} • Improper valve timing due to jumping out of timing chain {: #wp1060060} • Contaminated MAF sensor {: #wp1060070} • Improper engine compression {: #wp1060080} • Exhaust system clogging {: #wp1060090} Warning(See } The following troubleshooting flow chart contains fuel system diagnosis and repair procedures. Read following warnings before performing fuel system services: {: #wp1062251} • Fuel vapor is hazardous. It can easily ignite, causing serious injury and damage. Always keep sparks and flames away from fuel. {: #wp1060110}• Fuel line spills and leakage are dangerous. Fuel can ignite and cause serious injuries or death and damage. Fuel can also irritate skin and eyes. To prevent this, always complete "BEFORE SERVICE PRECAUTION" and "AFTER SERVICE PRECAUTION" described in this manual. {: #wp1060124BEFORE SERVICE PRECAUTION [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060142}(See AFTER SERVICE PRECAUTION [L8, LF].) {: #wp1060156} Caution} • Disconnecting/connecting quick release connector without cleaning it may possibly cause damage to fuel pipe and quick release connector. Always clean quick release connector joint area before disconnecting/connecting, and make sure that it is free of foreign material. {: #wp1062252
