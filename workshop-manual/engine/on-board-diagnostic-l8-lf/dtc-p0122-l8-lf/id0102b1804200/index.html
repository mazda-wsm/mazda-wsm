DTC P0122 [L8, LF]

id0102b1804200

{: #wp1059779}

DTC P0122 }{: #wp1061483

TP sensor No.1 circuit low input {: #wp1059842}

DETECTION CONDITION {: #wp1059782}

• If the PCM detects that the TP sensor No.1 voltage is below 0.1 V while the engine is running, the PCM determines that the TP sensor No.1 circuit has a malfunction. {: #wp1059784} Diagnostic support note} • This is a continuous monitor (CCM). {: #wp1059924} • The MIL illuminates if the PCM detects the above malfunction conditions during first drive cycles. {: #wp1059934} • PENDING CODE is available if the PCM detects the above malfunction condition. {: #wp1059944} • FREEZE FRAME DATA is available. {: #wp1059954} • The DTC is stored in the PCM memory. {: #wp1059964

POSSIBLE CAUSE {: #wp1059857}

• TP sensor No.1 malfunction {: #wp1059860} • Connector or terminal malfunction {: #wp1059996} • Short to ground in wiring harness between throttle body terminal F and PCM terminal 2AK {: #wp1060006} • Open circuit in wiring harness between throttle body terminal F and PCM terminal 2AK {: #wp1060016} • Open circuit in wiring harness between throttle body terminal E and PCM terminal 2AO {: #wp1060026} • PCM malfunction {: #wp1060036}
