Control Case Disassembly Note

  1. Pry the seal open at the projection on the case using a flathead screwdriver or similar tool as shown in the figure, and then remove the control case.{: #wp1059363}

Oil Seal (extension housing) Removal Note

  1. Remove the oil seal using a flathead screwdriver as shown in the figure.{: #wp1059412}

Top Cover Disassembly Note

  1. Pry the seal open at the projection on the case using a flathead screwdriver or similar tool as shown in the figure, and then remove the top cover.{: #wp1059449}

Extension Housing Disassembly Note

  1. Remove the spring pin of the control lever using a pin punch in the figure.{: #wp1059496}

  1. Remove the spring pin of the control rod end using a pin punch in the figure.{: #wp1059523}

  1. Remove the extension housing component.{: #wp1059550}

*Note*{: #wp1059577}

• Pry open the seal at the projection on the case using a flathead screwdriver or similar tool as shown in the figure, and then remove the extension housing.

Oil Seal (control rod) Disassembly Note

  1. Using a flathead screwdriver, remove the oil seal as shown in the figure.{: #wp1059605}

Sealing Cap Disassembly Note

  1. Remove the sealing cap using the SST.

Caution

• Remove the sealing cap only if there is malfunction.
